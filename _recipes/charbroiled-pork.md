# Charbroiled Pork

### Ingredients:

- [ ] 1 lb pork shoulder
- [ ] 3-4 cloves garlic
- [ ] 1-2 green onions
- [ ] 2+ tbsp cilantro
- [ ] 1 tbsp sugar
- [ ] 2 tbsp fish sauce
- [ ] 2 tbsp soy sauce
- [ ] 1+ tbsp sesame oil
- [ ] 1 tsp pepper

### Directions:

- [ ] Marinate pork in crushed garlic for 4-8 hours.
- [ ] Then, marinate pork for 45 minutes in:
* onions
* cilantro
* sugar
* fish sauce
* soy sauce
* sesame oil
* pepper
