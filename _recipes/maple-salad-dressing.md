# Maple Salad Dressing

### Ingredients:

- [ ] 1 tsp granulated maple sugar or maple syrup
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] 1 tsp black pepper
- [ ] 1 tsp dried parsley
- [ ] 2 tbsp vinegar
- [ ] 5 tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 1 tbsp mayo
- [ ] 1 green onion, finely chopped

### Directions:

Combine all ingredients together in a glass jar, close and shake until the mixture is creamy.
