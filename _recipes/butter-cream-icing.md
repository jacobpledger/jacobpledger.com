# Butter Cream Icing

### Ingredients:

- [ ] 1/3 Cup Butter
- [ ] 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla
- [ ] 2 Cups Icing Sugar
- [ ] 1 or 2 Tablespoons Milk (or splash of coffee)

### Directions:

- [ ] Cream butter.
- [ ] Slowly beat in icing sugar.
- [ ] Add liquid until creamy enough to spread.

### Notes:

* Double recipe for cake.
