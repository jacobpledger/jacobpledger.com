# Crackle Top Peanut Butter Cookies

Makes 5 dozen

### Ingredients:

- [ ] 3/4 cup butter
- [ ] 3/4 cup sugar
- [ ] 3/4 cup brown sugar
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 3/4 cup peanut butter
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla
- [ ] 1.75 cups flour
- [ ] 1/2 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 1/2 tsp salt

### Directions:

- [ ] Preheat oven to 375â„‰
- [ ] Mix butter, sugar, and brown sugar
- [ ] blend in the egg, peanut butter, and vanilla
- [ ] add flour, baking soda, and salt
- [ ] Form into balls.
- [ ] Roll in sugar.
- [ ] Pinch with fork.
- [ ] Bake 375F for 10-12 minutes
